Description

This is a website created to inform people about Sea Turtles. Turtles face many difficulties in survival due to human pollution and extenuating circumstances. Human and natural activity has increased the Mortality rate of Sea Turtles. This website wil also explore solutions to problems that cause Sea Turtles to become endangered.
